A.I. BODY FAT PERCENTAGE CALCULATOR

The A.I. Body Fat Percentage Calculator is a visual body fat calculator that can be used to estimate your total body fat percentage with just a picture of yourself. For the body fat calculator to work, you must upload a picture of yourself.

In the picture you upload, you must stand straight, flex and be the only one in it. Men must be shirtless and women in gym attire/form fitting clothing.

You can use a bathroom selfie or ask someone to take a picture of yourself. Make sure the image is a close-up picture. The picture you upload must follow these rules, otherwise, the results will be greatly skewed.


When you upload your picture, a neural network (A.I.) gives you an answer within 5 seconds (based on the closest body fat percentage range). It is important to remember that this number is just an estimate.

If you need help in losing extra fat, check out the Keto Ebook, Audio and Video Series. It will show up below after you upload a picture.
