What's Estimate Body Fat?

Estimate Body Fat is a collection of fitness tools, and articles designed to help you track your fitness, get stronger, look better and stay healthier.

Our Body Fat/Fitness Measurement Tools include traditional text based calculators and modern image based calculators.

Each calculator has been designed to make it as easy as possible to obtain your body fat percentage. A good start would be to try out our text based calculators first and then move onto image based ones. However, it is totally left upto whichever one you feel comfortable using.
